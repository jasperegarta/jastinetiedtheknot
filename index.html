    Our Story

    June 2019

    It all started at work in June 2019 when two paths crossed in the most unexpected way. I was working as a Timekeeper, and Jasper was working as an IT in the same company. We were teased by our colleagues, and then we were finally introduced. Although neither of us was looking for love at the time, we couldn't help but notice the instant connection. Our first conversation was effortless, filled with laughter, shared interests, and a spark that we both knew we couldn’t ignore.

    July 2019

    After a few weeks of getting to know each other and talking on the phone, we decided to go on our first official date. We went to Greenfield in Mandaluyong and decided to have a few drinks while listening to the beautiful songs that were being played. We were so happy, laughing at each other, and sharing stories about our past. It was an evening we’ll never forget, and we both left feeling like we'd known each other forever.

    December 2023

    Our six-year journey has been a roller coaster. In December 2023, we faced our first serious challenge - we lost our first baby. We were so excited and eager to become parents. It broke our hearts and devastated us, but through it all, we chose to stay together. In that painful moment, we realized just how deeply we love each other, and we held on to our faith that God would bless us in His perfect time. In August 2024, God tested us again - we lost our second baby. This time, we felt our faith shaking, but still, we chose to believe that everything happens for a reason. We trust that God has better plans for us and that something beautiful is already being prepared. Our journey toward forever hasn’t been easy, but it’s been filled with love, growth, and countless cherished memories. As we take the next step toward our wedding day, we are overjoyed to celebrate this new chapter with you. Thank you for being part of our love story. We can’t wait to share our special day with the people we cherish most.
